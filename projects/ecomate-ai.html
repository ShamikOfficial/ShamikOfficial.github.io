EcoMate-AI

The problem

Most people have no idea what their daily choices cost the planet, and sustainability tools demand tedious manual entry. The challenge: make environmental impact effortless to understand from the messy data people already have - receipts, photos, and activity.

What I built

  • A multimodal agentic platform on Gemini 2.5 Flash that ingests text, images, and structured data.
  • Agent workflows that map inputs to impact and generate personalized sustainability recommendations.
  • A live, demo-ready app shipped within the 24-hour hackathon window.

Outcome

  • Won 1st place in the sustainability track against an industry judging panel.
  • Validated a fast multimodal-to-recommendation pipeline that turns raw inputs into actions.
