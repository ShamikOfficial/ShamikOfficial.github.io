Smooth Operation

Turns Atlassian workflow chaos into clarity - real-time flow graphs and heatmaps surface blockers, risks, and engagement so teams collaborate faster and with less friction.

View on Devpost

The problem

Across Jira and Confluence, work state is scattered and hard to read at a glance. Teams can't easily see where things are stuck, which work is risky, or where engagement is dropping - so blockers hide until they hurt.

What I built

  • Real-time flow graphs that visualize how work moves through an Atlassian workspace.
  • Heatmaps that highlight blockers, risk, and engagement hot spots at a glance.
  • An interface designed to help teams collaborate faster and without friction.
